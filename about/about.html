What is EmpowerED?

EmpowerED is a festival that is hosted annually to raise awareness for mental health, specifically more towards eating disorders. We encourage the youth to learn about stories, recoveries, and what an eating disorder even is along the way. The main goal of EmpowerED is to empower the youth towards self-care and self-acceptance, hoping that they too will empower others as well.
